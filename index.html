Dodge Game

  • It is a simple game to dodge the bird by waking it up and make it fly.
  • If nothing is done, the bird will fall back to sleep and lower its' altitude.
  • If the bird is hit by the fire balls coming toward it, the game is over.
  • The fire balls gets faster as time passes.
  • The game can always restart after the game over, but not during the play.
  • The upper right side of screen will show the time of survival and game over screen will show the record.
  • The fire balls always come out within the screen so if the bird goes above the screen, it won't get a hit.
  • The bird can go up above the screen, but does not infinitly increase its' height.
  • The color range of fire balls can be changed with 'Settings' and they are shown on the upper rightside of the screen.
